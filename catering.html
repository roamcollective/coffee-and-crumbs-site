How to book catering

For now, we keep it personal. To start a catering order:

1. Visit our Square site and start an order.
2. Use the notes section to mention "catering" and your event date & headcount.
3. We'll follow up to confirm details, pickup time, and any special requests.

You can also reach out via Instagram DM at @coffeeandcrumbsva for questions before you place an order.
