Our Story

From El Salvador to Northern Virginia

Coffee & Crumbs was born in a cozy Northern Virginia kitchen, but its heart is in El Salvador. Our family recipes, love for coffee, and memories of Salvadoran bakeries shape everything we serve.

Every cup is handcrafted, every bake is made in small batches, and every order is a little piece of home shared with our community.

Whether you grew up with quesadilla salvadoreña or you’re trying it for the first time, we’re here to make your coffee break feel warm, welcoming, and a little bit special.
