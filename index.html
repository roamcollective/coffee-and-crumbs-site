Coffee & Crumbs was born from a deep love for Salvadoran culture and the art of handcrafted coffee. We bring the rich flavors and warm traditions of El Salvador to Northern Virginia, one cup at a time.

Every coffee is made by hand with care and intention. Our Salvadoran pastries follow recipes passed down through generations. Every baked good is fresh from our kitchen to your table.
